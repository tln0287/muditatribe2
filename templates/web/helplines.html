Arpita Foundation

Arpita foundation is a non-profit organisation providing professional guidance and counselling to callers from various parts of India to resolve personal and psychological problems.
One of the objectives of the Foundation is the Helpline Service predominantly to extend our helping hand to the callers who feel helpless, hopeless and worthless due to various reasons who were initially assisted by a small group of counsellors who have over a decade of experience in the field of counselling psychology. This growing force is now a team of dedicated and trained counsellors/volunteers who are certified Gate Keepers in Suicide Prevention.
Our team comprises of Counselling Psychologists counsellors/volunteers who work between 7am to 9pm with 14 volunteers serving per day.

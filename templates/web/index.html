The Joy of Giving Others Happiness

Welcome to Mudita, where the very essence of joy resides in the act of bestowing happiness upon others. In a world where some believe anxiety and depression can be vanquished with a mere act of will, we aim to reveal that these afflictions often feel like a battle in the tempestuous sea. You fight, sometimes alone, until a safety boat emerges.
